Our story

The story behind Call Electric

CALL Electric is a professional electrical contracting business based in Lewisburg, WV, serving Greenbrier County and the surrounding areas. We specialize in a wide range of electrical services, including panel mapping, electrical upgrades, surge protection, whole-house wiring, generators and generator inlet boxes with interlock kit. Whether you’re a homeowner or a small business owner, CALL Electric is dedicated to delivering reliable, efficient, and high-quality solutions tailored to your needs.

At CALL Electric, we combine advanced technology with expert craftsmanship to ensure every project meets the highest standards of safety and performance. When you choose CALL Electric, you’re choosing a partner committed to customer satisfaction, precision, and professionalism.

Chris Ratliff, the owner and operator of CALL Electric, brings decades of experience and dedication to every project. He spent 24 years at Collins Aerospace, where he developed expertise in complex electrical systems and advanced technologies. As a licensed master electrician, Chris has built a reputation for excellence in the field, leveraging his technical skills and industry knowledge to provide exceptional service.

In addition to his professional credentials, Chris is an Army veteran and a lifelong resident of Greenbrier County. His commitment to hard work, integrity, and community involvement defines his approach to both his business and personal life.

Chris has been happily married to his wife, Amy, for many years. He is a proud father of two daughters and a grandfather to one grandson. When he's not working, Chris loves spending quality time with his family and giving back to the community he cherishes.
