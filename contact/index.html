Frequently asked questions

Answers to Your Most Common Electrical Questions

What types of electrical services do you offer?

We provide a range of electrical services including panel mapping, electrical upgrades, surge and lightning protection, backup generator installation, whole-house wiring, and receptacle/switch installation for residential and small commercial properties.

How do I know if I need an electrical upgrade?

Signs you may need an upgrade include frequent tripped breakers, flickering lights, outdated electrical panels, or the need for additional outlets. We offer free consultations to evaluate your system and recommend upgrades if needed.

Is it safe to attempt electrical repairs myself?

Electrical work can be dangerous if you're not trained. It's always best to hire a licensed professional to ensure your safety and avoid potential hazards like electrical fires or shock.

Do you offer emergency electrical services?

Yes, we offer emergency electrical services. If you're experiencing an electrical issue that needs immediate attention, don’t hesitate to contact us, and we’ll respond as quickly as possible.
