Backup Generator Installation

Install backup generators to keep your home or business powered.

About the service:

Our backup generator installation ensures your home or business stays powered during electrical outages. We provide full-service installation, from selecting the right model for your needs to the final setup and testing. We also install generator inlet boxes and interlock kits to safely back-feed portable generators. With a reliable backup generator, you’ll never have to worry about losing power again, whether due to storms, outages, or emergencies.

What’s Included?

  • Professional consultation to assess your power needs
  • Expert installation of Generac backup generators
  • Installation of generator inlet boxes and interlock kits for safe portable generator use
  • System testing to ensure optimal performance
  • Ongoing maintenance and support options for peace of mind
